Recipe

Ingredient Amount
Broccoli Two heads
Carrots 4 sticks
celery 4 sticks
Onions 2 whole
Philadelphia cream cheese 1/2 tub
Vegetable stock 1 cube
Water 3/4 of pot
  1. Chop the Carrots, Onions and celery.
  2. Gently fry the chopped carrots, onions and celery in the pot.
  3. Add boiling water to the pot to fill it 3 quarters full and add the vegetable stock cube.
  4. Boil the pot for 20 minutes on medium heat
  5. Chop the heads of the broccoli.
  6. Add the chopped broccoli to the pot and boil for another ten minutes.
  7. Stop cooking and add the Philadelphia cream cheese.
  8. Blend the contents of the pot together until smooth.
